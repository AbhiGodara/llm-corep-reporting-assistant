CAPITAL REQUIREMENTS REGULATION (CRR) - PRA RULEBOOK EXTRACT

ARTICLE 26 - COMMON EQUITY TIER 1 ITEMS
1. Common Equity Tier 1 capital shall consist of the sum of the following elements:
   (a) capital instruments;
   (b) related share premium accounts;
   (c) retained earnings;
   (d) accumulated other comprehensive income;
   (e) other reserves;
   (f) funds for general banking risk.

2. Minority interests may be included in Common Equity Tier 1.

ARTICLE 36 - DEDUCTIONS FROM COMMON EQUITY TIER 1
Institutions shall deduct the following from Common Equity Tier 1 items:
(a) goodwill;
(b) other intangible assets;
(c) deferred tax assets that rely on future profitability.

ARTICLE 51 - ADDITIONAL TIER 1 CAPITAL
1. Additional Tier 1 capital comprises capital instruments that:
   (a) are issued and paid-up;
   (b) are subordinated to creditors;
   (c) are not redeemable at the initiative of the holder;
   (d) have no fixed maturity date.

ARTICLE 61 - TIER 2 CAPITAL
Tier 2 capital comprises capital instruments and subordinated loans that:
(a) are issued and paid-up;
(b) have an original maturity of at least five years;
(c) are not redeemable before maturity without prior supervisory approval.

ARTICLE 72 - TOTAL OWN FUNDS
Total own funds = Common Equity Tier 1 + Additional Tier 1 + Tier 2 capital.
All values must be reported in thousands of reporting currency.
