1. Common Equity Tier 1 (CET1) capital includes ordinary share capital,
retained earnings, and other comprehensive income, after regulatory adjustments.

2. Additional Tier 1 (AT1) capital consists of qualifying perpetual instruments
that absorb losses on a going concern basis.

3. Tier 2 capital includes subordinated instruments with a minimum original
maturity of five years.

4. Total Own Funds must equal the sum of CET1, AT1, and Tier 2 capital.

COREP Own Funds Template Instructions (Simplified)

- OF_010: Report CET1 capital.
- OF_020: Report AT1 capital.
- OF_030: Report Tier 2 capital.
- OF_040: Report Total Own Funds.

All reported values must be non-negative and internally consistent.
